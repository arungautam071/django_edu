About Us

Quality Education For Everyone

Founded in 2017,Number Crunching is a one-stop solution devoted to the benefit of students seeking to make mathematics their forte. Its mission is to promote interest and enhance lucidity and skill in the subject. Fear towards mathematics is quite prevalent, but number crunching ensures to shun every kind of fear there is towards maths. At its early stages, it covered only the CBSE curriculum but now boasts a wide range of courses pertaining to the ICSE & IB curricula as well as preparation for competitive examinations
